What this layer buys the platform

Eleven distinct moments, eight actors, none of them a teacher — mapped in full because the teacher relationship can’t be trusted any further than this layer actually holds.
Governance & oversight
A chain that can back up a safety claim, not just state one — still mostly theorized, worth building out first.
Platform operations
Records, subscriptions, and content that stay correct without a teacher ever noticing the work behind it.
Marketplace & supply
A vetting relationship already evidenced with one real vendor — the clearest proof point in this entire map.
