The layer behind the teacher

Eight actors, none of whom touch a classroom directly, grouped into the three functions that make the teacher relationship possible.

A teacher’s trust is only as solid as what’s holding it up. This map goes deep on the three functions that hold it up — governance, operations, and marketplace supply — the same way the teacher map goes deep on the teacher’s own moments.
FunctionActorsWhat it makes possible for the teacher
Governance & OversightEDB, Principal, data-governance gatekeeperA safety claim the teacher can actually rely on, not just hear
Platform OperationsAdmin/IT, EdCity SMS team, EdCity content teamRecords, subscriptions, and content that stay correct without the teacher noticing
Marketplace & SupplyEdMarket gatekeeper, VendorA tool library the teacher can browse with confidence
Every panel below groups its actors by what they jointly enable, not by individual actor rings — the point of this map is depth on three functions, not breadth across eight actors treated separately.
